# Introduction

I am a fifth year astronomy graduate student working with Dr. Eileen Friel at
[Indiana University](http://www.astro.indiana.edu/). In my research, I enjoy
exploring what open and globular clusters in the Milky Way can teach us about
the history and evolution of our Galaxy and stellar populations, using both
observations and computer simulations. I am the co-developer of the Python
based pipeline [odi-tools](https://bjanesh.github.io/odi-tools/) used to
process and stack images from the One Degree Imager (ODI) on the
[WIYN](https://www.noao.edu/wiyn/) 3.5-meter telescope.

I am also very interested in learning and applying data mining and machine
learning techniques to research problems in astronomy.

In my free time I enjoy attempting to be a blues guitarist, long distance
running, and following Manchester United.
